ABOUT US

Global Dimension Company, Inc. is a private company based in Cebu, Philippines. It was founded by Archt. Andrei Nicolai Chang in 2008.

Exquisite INTERIOR Fit-Outs

Specializing in innovative and fully comprehensive solutions for interiors and customized cabinetries, Global Dimension has achieved a reputation of providing a wide range of services for contracting projects from residential houses, condominium units, and commercial offices to high end restaurants all over the Philippines.

And work closely with our clients to translate their dreams into reality. With a team of creative designers and skilled workforce, our projects reflect ideas, keenness to details, quality and balance between cost and aesthetics.

We listen to the needs of individual client and offer advices from style preferences to pocket-friendly options. Our services run from concepts, site inspection for customization to supplying materials, as far as accessories are concerned.
